Travel Routine

What is the "day" routine of transportation system?
The number of Initiating and Terminating transports in each half hour represented by Radial Stacked Barchart. Hover around the graph to discover the numbers.The pick time is around 7-9 am morning and 4-7 pm evening, which is the time usually when most of the commutes happen.

Connectivity

How connected are the big train stations in different parts of the country?
The Edge bundling on map represents connectivity of stations by direct trips. You should hover around the map and stations to see the connections, which are defined by bundling edges.
Hover around the Basel, Geneve, Zurich, Lausanne to see the connectivity.

Capacity of stations in a day

How many transports are operating at stations in day time?
The Time-Interactive Map represents the stations and the number of vehicles passing through them.
Adjust and move the timebar to get information of the number of transports in stations.
The color of a station characterizes the type of operating transport in the station.
